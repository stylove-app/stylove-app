Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Stylove (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Stylove mobile application and related services.

Information we may collect

  • Account information — such as your email address and authentication identifiers when you create or sign in to an account.
  • Wardrobe items — clothing entries you add to your private wardrobe, including labels and style details you provide.
  • Photos — images you upload of your clothing or outfits. These are used to build and personalise your wardrobe experience.
  • Style preferences — moods, occasions, saved looks, and similar choices you make in the app.
  • Location and weather — with your permission, we may use approximate location to suggest looks suited to your city and current weather.
  • Purchase status — subscription or premium entitlement information to provide the correct access level.
  • Usage and diagnostics — app interactions, crash reports, and performance data to improve reliability and experience.

How we use information

We use your information to operate Stylove, personalise outfit suggestions, maintain your wardrobe, process subscriptions, improve the app, and respond to support requests. We do not sell your personal information.

Service providers

We use trusted third-party services to run Stylove, including:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and secure storage.
  • OpenAI — processing to generate outfit suggestions and related styling content.
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management.
  • Sentry — error monitoring and diagnostics.
  • PostHog — product analytics to understand how the app is used.

These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms and privacy policies. We share only what is necessary for them to perform their services.

Data retention and security

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your choices

You may update profile information in the app, manage location permissions in your device settings, and request account deletion through the app or by contacting us. You may also cancel subscriptions through your App Store account settings.

Children

Stylove is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email support@stylove.app.
